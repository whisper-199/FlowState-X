Deploy guide
Free forever. No credit card. Live in under 10 minutes.
1
Push to GitHub
Repo structure:
vercel.json package.json
api/auth.js api/callback.js
api/tweet.js api/logout.js
public/index.html
2
Create X app at developer.x.com
Go to developer.x.com, create an app with Read + Write permissions. Set the callback URL to https://YOUR-DOMAIN.vercel.app/api/callback
3
Import on Vercel + set env vars
Go to vercel.com/new, import your repo. In Settings → Environment Variables add:
TWITTER_API_KEY
TWITTER_API_SECRET
SESSION_SECRET (any long random string)
4
Deploy & sign in
Click Deploy. Visit your live URL, go to X Account, click Sign in with X — done. Any visitor can now authorise their own X account.
Completely free: Vercel Hobby plan + X Free tier is enough for personal use. No credit card required on either platform.

Timing
Post when your audience is awake
The best windows are 8–10am and 6–9pm in your audience's timezone. Even great content dies at 3am.
Engagement
Reply within the first hour
The first 60 minutes determine reach. Reply to every comment — the algorithm treats replies as strong signals.
Content
Lead with the hook, not the context
Your first sentence must stop the scroll. Open with the most specific or bold sentence in the post.
Community
Comment before you post
Spend 15 minutes leaving thoughtful replies on bigger accounts in your niche. It drives organic follows.
Content
Use threads for complex ideas
Threads get significantly more reach. Tease the conclusion in tweet 1 and deliver at the end.
Engagement
Pin your best post
Your pinned post is the first thing new visitors see. Make it show who you are and why they should follow.
Timing
Consistency beats virality
Accounts posting every day for 90 days grow 10x faster than those chasing one big moment.
Community
Find your micro-niche
Be specific — "the developer who writes about startup failures" attracts a loyal, engaged following.
Content
Share opinions, not just facts
Facts get nodded at. Opinions spark conversations. Tell people what you think news means.
